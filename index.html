FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

Which countries are currently supported?
PayRu is currently live in Nigeria. We're expanding to Ghana, South Africa, Rwanda and Pakistan — join the waitlist and we'll notify you when we go live in your region.
What local currencies can I receive?
Currently we settle in Nigerian Naira (NGN). As we expand, you'll be able to receive Ghanaian Cedi (GHS), South African Rand (ZAR), Rwandan Franc (RWF) and Pakistani Rupee (PKR) — paid directly to your local bank account.
What identification documents do I need?
You can verify using any valid national ID — NIN, Voter's Card (PVC), Driver's License or International Passport, depending on what's accepted in your country.
How long does a cross-border settlement take?
Once your crypto deposit is confirmed and your bank details are on file, settlements are typically credited to your local bank account within 15 minutes across all supported countries.
Which cryptocurrencies can I convert?
We currently support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), BNB and USD Coin (USDC), with more assets being added regularly.
Are there any fees or hidden charges?
Our exchange rate already reflects our service fee — the local currency amount you see at the start is exactly what you'll receive. No surprise deductions, no hidden charges.
