FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

Which countries are currently supported?
PayruExchange currently supports Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Rwanda and Pakistan — with more countries being added regularly. If your country isn't listed yet, join our waitlist and we'll notify you when we go live in your region.
What local currencies can I receive?
You can receive your settlement in Nigerian Naira (NGN), Ghanaian Cedi (GHS), South African Rand (ZAR), Rwandan Franc (RWF) or Pakistani Rupee (PKR) — paid directly to your local bank account.
What identification documents do I need?
You can verify your identity using any valid national ID from your country — including National ID Numbers, Driver's License, Passport or Voter's Card, depending on what's accepted in your region.
How long does a cross-border settlement take?
Once your crypto deposit is confirmed and your bank details are on file, settlements are typically processed and credited to your local bank account within 15 minutes — across all supported countries.
Which cryptocurrencies can I convert?
We currently support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), BNB and USD Coin (USDC), with more crypto assets being added regularly.
Are there any fees or hidden charges?
Our exchange rate already reflects our service fee — the local currency amount you see at the start of your settlement is exactly what you'll receive in your bank. No surprise deductions, no hidden charges.
