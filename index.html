FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

Can I buy crypto on PayruExchange, or only sell?
You can do both. Fund your account with Naira to buy Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum and other supported assets at competitive rates, or sell your crypto for an instant payout to your bank account — all from the same dashboard.
How do I sell my crypto on PayruExchange?
Create an account, complete your KYC verification, then head to your dashboard and click "Sell Crypto." Enter the amount you want to sell, choose your asset, and follow the prompts to send your crypto and receive your payout.
What identification documents do I need?
You can verify your identity using your National ID Number (NIN), Voter's Card (PVC), Driver's License or International Passport.
How long does a payout take?
Once your crypto deposit is confirmed and your bank details are submitted, payouts are typically processed within 15 minutes during business hours.
Which cryptocurrencies are supported?
We currently support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), BNB and USD Coin (USDC), with more assets being added regularly.
Are there any fees?
Our exchange rate already reflects our service fee — the amount you see at checkout is exactly what you'll receive, with no surprise deductions.
