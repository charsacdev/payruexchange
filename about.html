About PayruExchange

The Global Platform Turning Crypto Into Local Currency

We're on a mission to make cross-border crypto settlements fast, transparent, and accessible to everyone — across Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Rwanda, Pakistan and beyond.

Our Story

Built for the global generation of crypto users

PayruExchange was founded by a team of traders and builders who saw a real problem: getting crypto into your local bank account across borders was slow, expensive, and opaque. We built the platform we always wished existed — one that handles the full cross-border settlement, end to end.

Today, PayruExchange serves over 50,000 users across Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Rwanda and Pakistan — offering competitive real-time exchange rates, secure KYC-backed accounts, and local currency payouts that land in your bank within minutes.

Whether you're a freelancer getting paid in USDT, a trader cashing out, or simply converting savings — we make cross-border settlements completely effortless.
