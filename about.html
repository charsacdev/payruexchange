About PayruExchange

Helping You Turn Crypto Into Real Value

Our Story

Built by traders, for traders

PayruExchange was founded by a team of crypto traders who got tired of slow, unreliable off-ramps and unclear exchange rates. We set out to build a platform that does one thing exceptionally well: convert your digital assets into cash, quickly and transparently.

Today, PayruExchange serves tens of thousands of users across Nigeria, offering competitive rates, secure KYC-backed accounts, and payouts that land directly in your bank account — often within minutes.

Whether you're cashing out savings, getting paid for freelance work in crypto, or simply liquidating an investment, we're here to make the process effortless.
